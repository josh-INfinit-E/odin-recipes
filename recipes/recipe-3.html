Simple Salad

Overview

Vegetarian/Length 5 minutes/Servings 2/Prep Time 5 minutes/Difficulty 1/5

A simple yet satisfying salad. Nothing dramtic just some plain ol greens.

Ingredients

  • Lettace
  • Carrots
  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumbers
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Olive Olive
  • Balsamic Vinegar

Instructions

  1. Wash veggies
  2. Cut carrots, tomatoes, cucumbers into cubes
  3. Combine Olive Oil and Balsamic Vinegar into dish
  4. Combine all ingredents in large bowl mixing together
  5. Serve
